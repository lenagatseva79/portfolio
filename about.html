About me

Hi, I'm Lena Gatseva!

Front-end developer from Ukraine currently based in Germany

Like many developers, my path to programming wasn't exactly straightforward. I am a student at a medical university in Ukraine. When the war started in my country, I saw SheCodes bootcamp as a great opportunity to learn how to code.

When I finished first SheCodes Basics workshop with my very first project about Netflix series, I realized that I really enjoying coding. Then I decided to seriously get into programming.

I am so happy with all the knowledge I have gained and that I have discovered my passion for coding. Now I'm looking forward to starting my career as a Front-End developer.
